# User Guide

This guide explains how to use AetherFlow after installing it on Windows.

## First Launch

1. Open AetherFlow from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.
2. Wait for the download component check to finish.
3. If the status says the components are ready, the app can download videos and playlists.

## Change Language

1. Click the settings icon in the top-right corner.
2. Choose `Rus` or `Eng`.
3. The choice is saved automatically and stays after restart.

## Download a Video

1. Copy a YouTube video link.
2. Paste it into the link field.
3. Click `Check`.
4. Review the preview: title, channel, thumbnail, and duration.
5. Choose `Video (MP4)`.
6. Pick the quality.
7. Choose a save folder.
8. Click `Download`.

The item appears in the queue on the right. The queue shows progress, speed, ETA, and status.

## Download Audio

1. Paste and check a YouTube link.
2. Select `Audio (MP3)`.
3. Choose the save folder.
4. Click `Download`.

You can switch the audio container to `M4A` in the audio format selector.

## Download a Playlist

1. Paste a YouTube playlist link.
2. Click `Check`.
3. Review the playlist title and item count.
4. Choose format, quality, and save folder.
5. Click `Download`.

AetherFlow creates a separate folder using the playlist title and downloads the videos into it.

## Queue Controls

- `Cancel`: stops a queued or active download.
- `Cancel all`: stops all active and queued downloads.
- `Retry`: restarts a failed item.
- Playlist rows can be expanded or collapsed.

## Notes

- Some videos can be unavailable because of privacy, regional restrictions, or YouTube limitations.
- Download speed depends on YouTube, your connection, and the selected quality.
- Use AetherFlow only for content you have the right to download.
